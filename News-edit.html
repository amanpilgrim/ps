5000 poppies for Anzac Day 2015

As part of the World War I commemorations, the 5000 Poppies Project is aiming to knit, crochet and sew poppies for a display at the Wigram Air Force Museum on Anzac Day in 2015.

They have already collected over 5000 poppies and are now aiming for 18,000, one for every service-person who lost their life in World War I.

A group from within Parliament aims to contribute a big pile of parliamentary poppies for the project. On display in Parliament House are their first 100 poppies. Contributors include Debra Angus, Andie Lindsay, Lisa Kinloch, Kay Houlahan, Tracy Jacobs, Elizabeth Grant, Paula Williams, Tanna Lepou, Sue Macpherson, Lynda Blair, Michele Reardon, Carol Browuer and Ainslie Newell.

If you would like to knit or crochet a few poppies, please feel free to join the group who meet at lunchtime once a week. Contact andie.lindsay@parliament.govt.nz for more information.

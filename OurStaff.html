Everything has its own instruction manual these days, from microwaves to that flat pack furniture you still somehow managed to incorrectly assemble. But did you know Parliament has an owner's manual?

Radio New Zealand's most recent The House segment interviews three Clerks of the House of Representatives David McGee (1985-2007), Mary Harris (2007-2015), and David Wilson (2015- ) at the release of fourth edition of Parliamentary Practice in New Zealand the ultimate 'how-to' guide for Parliament. The three clerks (and co-authors) discuss the book, the job, and McGee's dedication to writing the original by hand.

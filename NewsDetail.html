    Next staff orientation tour

    Annual report18 June 2015
    Content

    Are you new to Parliament or not sure where to go?

    Visitor Services invite new and existing staff on a special 75-minute orientation tour of the parliamentary precinct on Monday 22 June at 9am.

    The tour will include key locations in Bowen House, Executive Wing, Parliament House and the Parliamentary Library.

    If you'd like to join us or have any questions please phone the Tour Desk on Ext. 9503 or email us at tour.desk@parliament.govt.nz for a booking.

    The tour starts at 9.00am, Ground floor of Bowen House (meet in the room opposite Oliver's) and the tour will finish in the Visitor Centre, Executive Wing.
